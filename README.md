# 🌄 Devbhoomi Darshan – The Soul of Uttarakhand

Welcome to **Devbhoomi Darshan**, a digital gateway to the land of gods – Uttarakhand.
This premium web experience brings together the serene beauty, spiritual depth, and cultural richness of the Himalayas into one immersive platform.

From sacred temples to breathtaking landscapes, every section is crafted to give users a cinematic journey through Devbhoomi.

---

## ✨ Key Features

🏔️ **Scenic Destination Explorer**
Discover iconic locations like Mussoorie, Nainital, Kedarnath, and more with visually rich sections.

🛕 **Spiritual Experience**
Explore famous temples and pilgrimage sites that define the spiritual identity of Uttarakhand.

🎥 **Immersive Visual Design**
High-quality images and smooth transitions create a cinematic browsing experience.

🌌 **Modern UI/UX**
Elegant layouts with clean design, smooth animations, and engaging user interaction.

📱 **Fully Responsive**
Optimized for all devices — mobile, tablet, and desktop.

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

**HTML5** – Structured and semantic content
**CSS3** – Styling, animations, and responsive design
**JavaScript** – Interactive elements and dynamic behavior

---

## 🚀 Experience Devbhoomi

Step into the divine land where nature meets spirituality.
From the peaceful ghats of Rishikesh to the majestic peaks of the Himalayas, Devbhoomi Darshan brings Uttarakhand to your screen like never before.
